id,usecase,flowOfEvents
basic_flow
1,searchArticle,The actor enter a keyword on the search field and clicks on the Search Button.
2,searchArticle,The system shows a progress bar.
3,searchArticle,After it has finished searching; the system views the list of articles which are relevant to the keyword being entered.

alternative_flows

extension_points
3a,orderByYear,The actor can change the view of articles based on Order by Year of articles publication.
3a.1,orderByYear,The actor clicks on Year menu
3a.2,orderByYear,The system refreshes the view and order the articles based on their publication year.
3b,orderByCitation,The actor can change the view of articles based on Order by Citation of articles publication.
3b.1,orderByCitation,The actor clicks on Citation menu
3b.2,orderByCitation,The system refreshes the view and order the articles based on their publication year.
3c,deleteArticle,The Submitter Delete his Article.
3c.1,deleteArticle,The Submitter clicks on Delete menu of respected article.
3c.2,deleteArticle,The system refreshes the view and remove the deleted article from the list.
3d,viewDetail,The actor can view detail of an article
3d.1,viewDetail,The submitter clicks on the article's title
3d.2,viewDetail,The system views the metadata of the article
3d.2a,deleteArticle,The submitter delete his article
3d.2b,editMetadata,The submitter edit metadata of his article
3d.2b.1,editMetadata,The submitter edit the corresponding field
3d.2b.2,editMetadata,The submitter click on the save button
3d.2b.2,editMetadata,The system return to the list
